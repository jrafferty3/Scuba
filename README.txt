Controls:
W: up
A: left
S: down
D: right
Q: ascend
E: descend
SHIFT: swim faster
Z: identify wildlife you are close to
	you can hit 'Z' a second time to get more information about the wildlife
R: display time remaining
T: display an arrow that points at the boat
Y: plays return to boat alert
U: plays oxygen low alarm
I: plays nitrogen levels high alarm
O: plays equipment damaged alarm
P: plays severe weather alarm
H: changes the ambient level
	the levels are low/medium/high
